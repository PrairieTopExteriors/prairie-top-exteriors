About Us

Prairie Top Exteriors was built on a simple idea — that speed and quality shouldn't be a trade-off. Born and based in Saskatchewan, we combine local values with professional-grade craftsmanship. Our team of seasoned installers brings years of experience to every project, ensuring your siding and roofing are done right — and done fast.

We started this company because we saw a need: homeowners and builders deserve reliable crews who show up, work hard, and deliver top-tier results without delays. That’s what we do — with pride, precision, and integrity.

Your home deserves nothing less than Prairie-built strength, topped with professional care.
